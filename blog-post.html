The Ultimate Guide to Macros

By Alex Mercer • Dec 12, 2023

Understanding macronutrients—protein, carbohydrates, and fats—is the cornerstone of any effective nutrition plan. Whether you want to build muscle, lose fat, or just feel better, macros matter.

1. Protein: The Building Block

Protein is essential for muscle repair and growth. Aim for 1.6g to 2.2g per kg of body weight if you are active.

2. Carbohydrates: The Fuel

Carbs are your body's primary energy source. Don't fear them; utilize them around your workouts for maximum performance.

3. Fats: The Regulator

Healthy fats are crucial for hormone production and brain function. Include avocados, nuts, and olive oil in your diet.
